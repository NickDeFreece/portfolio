Active Projects

  • Semantic Elements

    This project explores the purpose and use of the semantic tags that help keep HTML5 developers sane.


  • GDDNS Updater

    A simple Python script to update your dynamic DNS host on our favorite not evil platform.


  • Winner Dinner

    The Winner Dinner app picks dinner for you! It shows you a semi-random recipe based on the components selected. Currently supports 9 meats, 5 carbs, and 7 cuisines!


  • Grill Planner

    **COMING SOON** This app creates a timetable for your grilling activities given a list of items to cook. It will tell you when to put on and take off, when to flip, and even move to indirect heat.
