Welcome to Friesland Industries

We are an organization dedicated to putting stuff on the internet. Since 1988, our founder Nick DeFreece has been generating high content through a revolutionary process we like to call "life".

Having automated the oxygen distribution process powered by a very specialized component of the body's central neural network, humans are now free to focus on what really matters: stuff on the internet.

Whether it's for useful tools, cat videos, unsolicited opinions, or beyond - Friesland Industries' promise to you is this: We will keep converting O2 and N2 to CO2, and we will keep consuming and/or creating stuff on the internet.
