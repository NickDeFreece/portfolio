Welcome to Friesland Industries

Our Mission

We are an organization dedicated to putting stuff on the internet. Since 1988, our founder Nick DeFreece has been generating high quality content through a revolutionary process we like to call "life".

Having automated the oxygen distribution process powered by a very specialized component of the body's central neural network, humans are now free to focus on what really matters: stuff on the internet.

Whether it's for useful tools, cat videos, unsolicited opinions, or beyond - Friesland Industries' promise to you is this: We will keep converting O2 and N2 to CO2, and we will keep consuming and/or creating stuff on the internet.


The People of Friesland

Chief Executive Officer: Nala DeFreece

The eldest and the wisest, Nala "B" DeFreece has been calling the shots for 14 years running and shows no signs of slowing. Not one to be hands-off, one can expect her to show up to supervise just about any project. And she will have opinions.

Firm but motherly, one might find a number of similarities to the NCIS character Leroy Jethro Gibbs. A long day of "boat work" on her fluffy pillow in the window won't stop her from powering through the night to find the truth (and the snacks). No countertop is off-limits. No half-full cup of water is safe.

When she's not punishing wrongdoers or mom-cleaning any other kitten that crosses her path, she can be found in any sunny spot - warming her tummy with her face in the shade.

Chief Financial Officer: Icis DeFreece

The keeper of the cuddles, this Chief Fluffy Officer won't let diabetes stop her from ensuring there's enough snugs for all. She boasts 10 years of having the biggest eyes we know!
